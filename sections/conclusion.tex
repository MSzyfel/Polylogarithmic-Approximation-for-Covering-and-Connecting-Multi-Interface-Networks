\section{Conclusion} \label{sec:conclusion}

In this paper, we have considered the problems \textsc{min-max} \coverage, \textsc{min-sum} \coverage and \textsc{min-max} \connectivity with heterogeneous costs, reintroducing some of the original enery model complexity from when the coverage problem was first introduced \cite{EnergeticPerformanceOfServiceOrientedMultiRadioNetworks}. As this is a generalization of the case with homogeneous costs, all innaproximability results for homogeneous costs transfer to the case with heterogeneous costs.
